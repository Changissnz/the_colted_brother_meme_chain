# The Colted Brother Meme Chain

A meme project featuring the same picture of a cartoon Colt .44 over a faded yellow background. 

Project started: 06/14/2026 
Project finished: 06/16/2026

Current events in American capitalism: 
- The markets are rough for the commoner.
- The cryptocurrency market somewhat caved in, and gold is now used as the "true" currency
  over the US Dollar.

The reason why not another background color? Why is yellow used as the background color for 
this meme chain? Thank Judas for that. Ubiquitous yellow. Valuable gold. And let's not forget, 
betrayal is a common theme during socio-economic times that encourage it. Me, personally? I 
started noticing the theme back in 2016, a decade ago. It spiked during the hayday of the 
COVID-19 pandemic, subsided and then rebounded starting after 2024. It's no biggie, though. 
Nothing personal. 

Yellow is one of those colors that grow on people. It's not a bold color. It's supposed to be 
those constant ubiquitous background colors. But once it achieves the wrong kind of influence, 
it turns unreliable. And that is sort of what wealth is prone to, since the market is the one 
that is supposed to divide. Once people do the dividing, it's more pro-active, and therefore 
less yellow. But the market is run by human beings. So there's that. My momentary thoughts on 
the color of yellow. 

In this project, I will post the same image, but with different captions along the same 
theme whenever I feel like it. And the same theme is the concept of brotherhood taking a 
turn for separation. 

The image was generated by ChatGPT. Everyone give credit to ChatGPT now! Yay! Go ChatGPT! 

I used `imgflip.com` to make the captions for the memes.
