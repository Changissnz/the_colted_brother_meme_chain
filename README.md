The date this project started: 06/14/2026 

Current events in American capitalism: 
- The markets are rough for the commoner.
- The cryptocurrency market somewhat caved in, and gold is now used as the "true" currency
  over the US Dollar.

The reason why not another background color? Why is yellow used as the background color for 
this meme chain? Thank Judas for that. Ubiquitous yellow. Valuable gold. And let's not forget, 
betrayal is a common theme during socio-economic times that encourage it. Me, personally? I 
started noticing the theme back in 2016, a decade ago. It spiked during the hayday of the
